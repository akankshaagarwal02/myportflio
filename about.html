What About Us

Leading the Way in Advanced Medical Care and Innovation.

SRR Hospital, located in AECS Layout A Block, Bangalore, is a premier healthcare facility dedicated to providing top-notch medical services. Established in 1999 as Shakti Healthcare Clinic and now evolving into Shakti Ram Reddy (SRR) Hospital, we serve a broad range of patients with a commitment to excellence and patient satisfaction. Our skilled professionals offer comprehensive care in areas such as dermatology, ENT, gynecology, general medicine, pediatrics, urology, orthopedics, and physiotherapy, ensuring accessible and high-quality healthcare for all.
